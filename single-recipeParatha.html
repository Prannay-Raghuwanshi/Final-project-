Paratha with Curd and Pickle:

Paratha is a popular Indian flatbread that can be stuffed with various fillings. In this recipe, we'll make plain whole wheat parathas and serve them with yogurt (curd) and pickle. The parathas are soft on the inside and crispy on the outside, making them a perfect accompaniment to the tangy yogurt and flavorful pickle.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

20 min.

instructions

Dry roast semolina until it turns golden brown. Set aside. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds. When they splutter, add curry leaves, chopped onions, and green chilies.
