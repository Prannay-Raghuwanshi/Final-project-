Vegetable Buddha Bowl

A colorful and nutritious bowl with a variety of veggies, grains, and a flavorful dressing.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

20 min.

instructions

Cook quinoa or rice according to package instructions. Roast chickpeas and vegetables with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Assemble bowls with quinoa, roasted veggies, chickpeas, and sliced avocado. Drizzle with tahini dressing.

ingredients

  • Quinoa or Brown Rice
  • Chickpeas
  • Assorted Roasted Vegetables (sweet potatoes, broccoli, carrots)
  • Avocado
  • Tahini Dressing
