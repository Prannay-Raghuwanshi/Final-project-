Vegetable Biryani

Vegetable Biryani is a fragrant rice dish with mixed vegetables and aromatic spices.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

20 min.

instructions

Saut√© onions until golden, add tomatoes, and cook until softened. Add mixed vegetables and biryani masala. Layer partially cooked rice and vegetable mixture in a pot. Add ghee, cashews, and raisins. Cook on low heat until rice is done.

ingredients

  • Basmati Rice
  • Mixed Vegetables (Carrots, Peas, Beans)
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Biryani Masala
  • Ghee
  • Cashews and Raisins
