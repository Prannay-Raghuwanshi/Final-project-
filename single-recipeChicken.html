Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken is a popular Indian dish with grilled chicken in a creamy tomato-based curry sauce.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

20 min.

instructions

Marinate chicken in yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and garam masala. Grill until cooked. In a separate pan, saut√© onions, add tomatoes and spices, cook into a thick sauce. Add grilled chicken and cream to the sauce. Simmer until flavors meld. Garnish with cilantro and serve with rice or naan.

ingredients

  • Chicken breast, cut into cubes
  • Yogurt
  • Garam masala
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Tomatoes
  • Cream
  • Onion
  • Cumin, coriander, and turmeric powder
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
