Chickpea Curry

A flavorful and nutritious vegetarian curry with chickpeas in a spiced tomato-based sauce.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

20 min.

instructions

Saut√© onions, garlic, and ginger. Add tomatoes and cook until soft. Add curry spices, coconut milk, and cooked chickpeas. Simmer until flavors meld. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with rice or naan.

ingredients

  • Chickpeas (canned or cooked)
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Tomatoes
  • Coconut Milk
  • Curry Spices (Cumin, Coriander, Turmeric)
  • Garam Masala
  • Fresh Cilantro for Garnish
