Masala Dosa

A thin, crispy rice crepe filled with a spiced potato mixture. It is often served with coconut chutney and tangy sambar (a lentil-based vegetable stew). Ingredients: Rice and urad dal batter, potatoes, onions, mustard seeds, curry leaves, turmeric, and spices.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

10 min.

instructions

Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds. When they splutter, add curry leaves and chopped onions. Saut√© until onions are golden brown. Add turmeric powder and mashed potatoes. Mix well. Season with salt and cook for a few minutes. Set aside. Heat a non-stick pan, pour a ladle of dosa batter, and spread it in a circular motion. Drizzle oil around the edges, cook until the dosa is crispy and golden brown. Place a portion of the potato mixture in the center, fold the dosa, and serve with coconut chutney and sambar.

ingredients

  • 1 cup rice and urad dal batter (fermented)
  • 2 large potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • A handful of curry leaves
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking
