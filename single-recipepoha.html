Poha

Flattened rice cooked with mustard seeds, curry leaves, turmeric, peas, and often garnished with coriander and lemon juice.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

10 min.

instructions

Rinse poha in water and set aside to soften. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds. When they splutter, add asafoetida and chopped onions. Saut√© until onions are translucent. Add peanuts, green peas, and turmeric powder. Cook for a few minutes. Add the softened poha, mix well, and cook until everything is heated through. Season with salt, garnish with coriander leaves, and serve with lemon wedges.

ingredients

    • 1 cup thick poha (flattened rice)
    • 1 onion, finely chopped
    • 1/2 cup green peas
    • 1/4 cup peanuts
    • 1 tsp mustard seeds
    • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
    • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
    • Salt to taste
    • Coriander leaves for garnish
    • Lemon wedges
